Whaling Scams

Whaling attacks, a widespread form of phishing, primarily focus on individuals with prominent positions or high-level targets, such as executives or decision-makers, aiming to exploit their authority or access to sensitive information for malicious purposes.

Whaling attacks are operated via sending an email. A good example is if you click on a link thinking it’s a website that you use like Facebook, Gumtree, Apple, etc and it asks you to install a malicious malware or sends you to a website that is impersonating a big website like Gumtree or Facebook.
